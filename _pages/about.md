I am a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at [UNIST](https://www.unist.ac.kr/), advised by Prof. Jaejun Yoo at the Lab of Advanced Imaging Technology (LAIT).

My research interests lie at the intersection of **generative models**, **federated learning**, **safety AI**, **loss landscape geometry**, and **weight-space learning**.

Previously, I received my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence from UNIST (2025) and my B.S. in Computer Science Engineering from Inha University (2022).
